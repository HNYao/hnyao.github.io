Yao ZHONG

PhD Student
MIRoC Lab, The University of Hong Kong

I am a PhD student in the Medical Intelligence and Robotic Cognition Lab (MIRoC) at The University of Hong Kong , working with Prof. Zhongliang Jiang.
I received my M.Sc. degree with distinction from The Technical University of Munich (Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence) and my B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University. During my master's studies, I was with the Mobile Robot Lab (MRL), supervised by Prof. Stefan Leutenegger and Hanzhi Chen, focusing on robot learning.
My favorite leisure hours are spent wandering the trails and catching the golden hour.
